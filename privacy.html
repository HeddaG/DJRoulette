Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026‑02‑22

DJ Roulette is designed for group play. We collect only what we need to run games and sync players across devices.

Information we collect

  • Player info: name and avatar you choose.
  • Device identifier: a random ID stored on your device to resume games.
  • Game data: queue items, votes, and scores for the games you join.

How we use data

We use this data to run multiplayer games, sync queues, and keep scores.

Third‑party services

We use Firebase (Firestore) to store game data. Music search/playback uses Apple Music and may include Spotify features where available. These services may process limited device or usage data required for their functionality.

Retention

Game data is temporary and automatically expires. You can also delete your account data in the app.

Account deletion

Settings → Account → Delete Account
